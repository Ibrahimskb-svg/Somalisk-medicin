Melatonin – hurdo

Waa warbixin kooban oo ku saabsan daawada – loogu talagalay waalidiinta iyo bukaannada Soomaaliyeed.

←Ku laabo liiska daawooyinka
1
Waa maxay Melatonin? (Hvad er det?)
  • Melatonin waa hormoon hurdada caawiya oo loo isticmaalo hurdo la’aanta.
2
Sida loo qaato (Dosering)
  • Qaado fiidkii, dhowr saac ka hor intaadan seexan ama 1–2 saac ka hor intaa seexan.
!
Digniino (Advarsler)
  • Waxay keeni kartaa hurdo badan, madax-wareer ama lallabbo khafiif ah.
3
Dhibaatooyinka (Bivirkninger)
  • Hurdo badan ama daal subaxeed.
  • Madax-xanuun ama wareer.
  • Calool-wareem ama lallabbo khafiif ah.
4
Is-dhexgalka daawooyinka (Interaktioner)
  • Daawooyinka hurdo-dejiya kale waxay kordhin karaan hurdo badan – la tasho dhakhtar ka hor isku darka.
  • Aalkoladu waxay dhaawacdaa hurdo tayadeeda; waa in laga fogaado marka la qaadanayo Melatonin.
  • Daawooyinka wadnaha qaarkood ama cadaadiska dhiigga waxay la falgali karaan Melatonin – dhakhtarkaaga u sheeg daawo kasta oo aad qaadato.
☾
Ramadaanka iyo daawada (Ramadan)
  • La hadal dhakhtarkaaga ka hor Ramadaanka si loo hubiyo waqtiga ugu habboon ee qaadashada Melatonin.
  • Badanaa waxaa la qaataa fiidkii (afurka kadib). Ha beddelin waqtiga adigoon la tashan dhakhtar.
⧉
Kaydinta (Opbevaring)
  • Ku hay daawada meel ka fog carruurta yar yar, kana hooseysa 25 °C.
  • Ku hay baakadka asalka ah si looga ilaaliyo iftiin iyo huur.
  • Ha qaadan daawada marka ay dhaafto taariikhda "EXP" (daawada goorta ee dhacday) ama haddii midabka isbedelay.
  • Wixii soo haray, weydii farmashiyaha sida loogu tuuro meesha ku habboon.
Haddii aad u baahan tahay faahfaahin dheeraad ah, la xiriir farmashiyaha ama dhakhtarkaaga.
